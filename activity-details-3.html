Itinerary

Flight to Bergamo. Arrival at 11:35 local time. Departure for a panoramic city tour with our private bus waiting for us at the airport after our arrival. The Milan Duomo, the second largest church in Italy, which took nearly 500 years to complete, the Fortezza Castle, and the Vittorio Emmanuele Gallery, which hosts the world's most famous fashion stores, are among the places to see. Transfer to your hotel after the tour. Overnight at your hotel.

After breakfast, we move to Venice. Possibility of participating in an extra Verona tour organized by our guide on the route (35 €). Romeo & Julietin house, Verona Arena, Scaligero Bridge with different architecture and Verona Cathedral are among the places to see in our tour. Departure to Venice after our Verona tour. After arriving at Tronchetto square, take a short ferry ride to St. Mark's Square and a panoramic city tour. On our city tour, the famous San Marco Square and Church, the Last Breath Bridge, where the prisoners look at Venice for one last time before entering the prison, the Grand Canal and the Rialto Bridge, the Palace of the Dukes, the Bell and Clock Tower and the Giorgio Maggiore Church are among the places to see. After the city tour, our guide will organize an extra Gondola tour (25 Euros), the symbol of Venice. Transfer to your hotel after the tour. Overnight at your hotel.

After breakfast, we are moving to Florence. Possibility to participate in an extra Bologna (25 €) tour, which our guide will organize on the route. On our Bologna city tour, we will see Piazza Maggiore (Grand Square), which is accepted as the center of Bologna, the Neptune Fountain just behind the Square, the Old Stock Exchange Building, Europe's first university building, the Quadrilatero (old market), the Garisenda that have become the symbols of Bologna and the asinelli towers. are between places. Departure to Florence after our tour. Panoramic city tour of Florence following arrival. On our tour, the Arno River that divides Florence into two, the Ponte Vecchio bridge famous for its jewelers, the Palazzo Vecchio where the Medici Family ruled Florence, Piazza Della Signoria, home to the masterpieces of the Renaissance, Hercules and David, The Santa Maria del Fiore Duomo with its dome and the famous Baptistery with its gate are among the places to see. At the end of the tour, transfer to your hotel in Montecatini. Overnight at your hotel.
